Services and Supplies for Your Convenience

3D Printer

We are pleased to announce that we can make 3D prints for you! Prints by our MakerBot are scheduled by appointment only. Please see more details at our help wiki or call to make an appointment.

Limitations:

Because of the way the Makerbot operates there are a number of limitations on what can actually be printed.

Print Size: The technical limit on how large an object can be printed is 10x10x10cm. However in actuality anything larger than about 8x8x8cm experiences dramatic loss of quality. Color: It can only print one color at a time. The colors we currently have are black, white, blue, florescent red, and glow in the dark. There are a number of methods people use to add more color to a print: sharpie, crayon, and acrylic paints have all been shown to work well. Overhangs: Because the Makerbot prints layer by layer from the bottom up everything needs to be printed directly on top of another layer. There is however some allowance for gentle slopes outward. If you have a question about whether or not your model will print properly feel free to consult with a member of the DMC's 3D Print Staff or refer to the Design Guidelines from Makerbot Industries.

How to get your model printed:

Make sure you have it saved as a .stl file. There are many tools available for free online that will convert 3D models to .stl files. If you need assistance converting your model feel free to contact a member of the DMC's 3D Print Staff.

Set up an appointment with a member of the DMC's 3D Print Staff. This is when you will work out the details of your print:

  • Finalizing the scale
  • Choosing a color
  • Discussing a printing timeline
  • Discussing the cost

    Consumables

    Batteries

    Some of our cameras, microphones, game controllers and other peripherals use non-rechargeable batteries. As a convenience to you, we offer these batteries for sale at reasonable prices. Many of our cameras use custom rechargeable batteries which will be fully charged when you borrow them

    Inkjet Printing and Paper

    We have a beautiful new inkjet printer and Epson papers for you to purchase. In order to ensure that your prints are color managed properly, we recommend only using Epson papers. If there is an Epson paper you would like to use that we do not carry, don't worry! We can create a printing profile just for you!

    T-shirt transfers and temporary tattoos

    We stock specialized transfer papers for iron-on t-shirt designs and water-based temporary tattoos. Have some fun creating a customized look for your friends or club.

    Create your own Buttons

    Use our heavy-duty button maker to design and make 2 inch pin-back buttons. Use photos, designs and text to convey your point of view and wear it proudly! We keep a small inventory of blank buttons stock however if you are planning a large project please order blanks in advance.
